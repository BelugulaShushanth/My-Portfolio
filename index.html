Employee Management System

Employee Management System is a web application developed to manage employees by a teamlead or a manager. Once the application is launched a manager can create a account and the login credentials are sent to his mail-id. Using those login credentials a manger can login to the the application and start adding employees under him. A manger can do all the CRUD operations on the employee and also a manager can assign tasks to the employee and monitor the status of the specific tasks. When a manager creates a new employee the login credentials for that employee are sent to the employee's mail-id and the employee can see the tasks asigned to him and also he can change the status of the task from TODO to InProgress or Done.

Tech Stack Used: Java, Spring Boot, Spring Rest, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap5
